Why Flow80

Built for ops teams.
Not developers.

Most automation tools were designed for developers. Flow80 was designed for the people who actually do the work — your ops team, your logistics coordinator, your office manager.

The difference

Your team actually runs it. Without tickets.

You don't need IT to build it.

With Flow80, your ops team builds and modifies workflows themselves. No dev tickets. No agency calls. You see a problem, you fix it. Same day, not next quarter.

It handles the edge cases.

Real operations work is messy. Data is missing. Systems go down. A supplier sends a purchase order in the wrong format. Flow80 doesn't break when this happens — it retries, adapts, and escalates only when it genuinely needs you.

You see what's happening.

No black boxes. Every workflow run is logged. Every step shows its input and output. When something goes wrong, you know exactly which step failed and why — not after the customer calls complaining.

It works with your existing tools.

Flow80 connects to the systems you already use — REST APIs, databases, file storage, email, EDI, and more. No rip-and-replace. No migration projects. Your systems stay; Flow80 sits in between them and makes them talk.
